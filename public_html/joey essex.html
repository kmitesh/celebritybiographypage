Joey Essex

Joey Donald Essex (born 29 July 1990) is an English television personality and DJ for Sugar Hut, a nightclub in Brentwood, England. He rose to fame in 2011 after appearing in the ITV2 television series The Only Way Is Essex. In 2013, Joey left the show, although his sister Frankie and cousin Chloe remain on it. In 2013, Joey took part in the first series of the celebrity diving show Splash! as a contestant.

 Career

Joey first appeared in The Only Way Is Essex as supporting cast in series 2 with his cousin Chloe Sims but was then promoted to main cast in series 3 where his sister, Frankie joined as supporting cast. His other cousin, Charlie Sims joined as supporting cast during the ninth series. In 2011, the TOWIE case recorded and released a single entitled "Reem": however, the song did not chart in the UK. He also featured in a song with the cast of TOWIE series 3, "Last Christmas", originally released by Wham! in 1984. The cover peaked at number 33 on the UK Singles Chart.
